The ADA Referral Network

Dr. Weiss and our clinical staff has been "in the business" for a long time, across a variety of settings, and have worked with a variety of interdisciplinary teams. As a result, we have an expansive referral network, both locally and nationally. To that end, there are a variety of related services that we can assist in getting our clients "hooked up" with, to connect with a variety of related experts and interventions. We can facilitate referrals for:

  • Appropriate medical & therapeutic treatments
  • Alternative and homeopathic medicine
  • Specialized nursing consultation & advocacy
  • Assistive Technologies (AT)
  • Augmentative & Alternative Communication (AAC)
  • Occupational Therapists with expertise in Sensory Integration (SI) Therapy
  • Music Therapy
  • Conventional Physical Therapy and related movement therapies ranging from Resistance Training, Hydrotherapy, Dance, Yoga, Drum Therapy to Therapeutic Metronome and Therapeutic Horseback riding
  • Relationship Directed Interventions (RDI) and/or Floor time
  • Legal advocates and Special Education Attorneys
